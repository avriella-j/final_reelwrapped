Troubleshooting

How do I download my Instagram activity logs?

1. Open Instagram on your mobile device
2. Go to your profile and tap the menu (three lines)
3. Tap "Your activity" or "Account"
4. Tap "Download your data" or "Download information"
5. Select "Some of your information" and choose activity data
6. Instagram will email you a download link (this may take up to 48 hours)

My upload is failing. What should I do?

- Ensure you're uploading a valid ZIP file from Instagram
- Check that the file size is under 100MB
- Try refreshing the page and uploading again
- Clear your browser cache and cookies
- Try using a different browser or device

I'm not seeing any analytics after uploading. Why?

- Make sure your activity logs contain sufficient data (likes, comments, follows)
- The processing may take a few minutes for large files
- Try refreshing the page
- Contact support if the issue persists

How do I update my profile information?

Go to your Profile page, click "Edit Profile", and update your bio, hashtags, or profile picture. Changes are saved automatically.

Can I delete my account and data?

Yes, you can request account deletion from your Profile page. All your data will be permanently removed from our servers.
